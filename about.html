About Ant Capital

Ant Capital connects discerning UAE angel investors with carefully selected, high-growth Chinese projects across F&B, education, STEAM, and smart retail — with real financial models, real teams, and real traction.

Our Mission

We bridge UAE capital with China's most promising early-stage ventures. Unlike traditional middlemen, we are operators on the ground — based in Zhuhai, China's Greater Bay Area — with direct access to deal sourcing, due diligence, and post-investment monitoring.

Every project we present comes with a detailed feasibility report, verified unit economics, and real operational data. We don't pitch ideas. We present operational businesses with proven financials.

Why Zhuhai?

Zhuhai is a Special Economic Zone in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area, one of China's most dynamic economic regions. It offers:

  • Strategic location connecting mainland China, Hong Kong, and Macau
  • Favorable business policies for cross-border investment
  • Growing ecosystem of F&B, education, and technology companies
  • Direct access to the broader Greater Bay Area market of 86M+ people
